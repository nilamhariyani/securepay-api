Securepay reduces the risk involved in financial transactions protecting both parties. It is a simple and transparent payment solution.
How Securepay works
1. Sign up to Securepay : To comply with anti-money laundering regulations we need to verify the details of both parties. The registration process only takes a few minutes. Once you have signed up you will have access to your own dashboard and be able to check the details of the transaction.
2. Fund the Escrow Account: Once you are happy with details you can deposit the agreed amount in the escrow account. Payment can be made by bank transfer or credit card. Securepay will then send an email to confirm receipt of funds to all parties. You will be able to keep track of the payment in real time on your dashboard.
3. Release Money: Once you are satisfied that the transaction is complete e.g. you have your goods or service release the funds via your Securepay dashboard. Securepay will then send an email to confirm release of funds to all parties.
